Privacy Policy for Airplay Camera

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Airplay Camera (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use our iOS application Airplay Camera (the “App”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your data on your device.

If you have any questions, please contact us at: support-aircam@digixone.com

Summary

  • We do not collect, receive, or store any personal data from your device.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • Photos, videos, and audio captured through the app remain on your device unless you choose to share or back them up.
  • Any diagnostic data shared with us is optional and anonymized/aggregated via Apple’s systems if enabled in your device settings.
  • No account, no tracking: We do not create user accounts or track you across apps or websites.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store your data. Specifically:

  • We do not access or transfer your photos, videos, or audio off your device.
  • We do not collect identifiers, contact information, or precise location.
  • We do not create user profiles or track you across apps or websites.

The App may request system permissions (e.g., camera, microphone, photo library, local network for AirPlay) solely to provide on-device functionality. You can control these permissions in iOS Settings.

Optional Diagnostics (If Enabled by You/System)

  • App Usage & Crash Logs: If you have opted in to share analytics and diagnostics with developers through iOS, Apple may provide us anonymized and aggregated crash reports and performance metrics. We do not receive content of your media or personal identifiers through these reports.

How We Use Information

  • On-Device Only: Your media and usage remain on your device. The App uses permissions strictly to enable features like capturing media and streaming via AirPlay.
  • Diagnostics: If available through Apple’s developer tools and only with your system-level consent, anonymized diagnostics may be used to fix crashes and improve performance.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not get your personal information.
  • We do not share your photos, videos, audio, or identifiers with third parties.
  • We may receive anonymized diagnostics from Apple if you opt in at the system level. We may disclose information if required by law.

Data Storage and Security

  • On-Device Storage: Airplay Camera does not upload or store your media on our servers. All media remains on your device unless you explicitly share or export it.
  • No Server Storage: We do not maintain user accounts or cloud databases for your content.
  • Security: We implement reasonable safeguards and rely on iOS security for on-device data protection. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Airplay Camera is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices and Controls

  • Permissions: Manage camera, microphone, photo library, and local network permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Analytics/Diagnostics: Control system analytics sharing in iOS Settings.
  • Deleting Data: Deleting the App removes its local data on your device. Any content you exported or shared outside the App is not affected.

International Users

Airplay Camera does not transfer your personal data to our servers. If optional diagnostics are shared through Apple’s systems, those may be processed according to Apple’s policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post updates in the App and revise the Effective Date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
